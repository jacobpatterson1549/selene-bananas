{{.ApplicationName}} is based on Bananagrams. To play, use tiles to form vertical and horizontal words that share letters. When a player runs out of tiles and all are connected, the snag button should be clicked to get another tile. This also gives all other players a new tile. If a player cannot use a tile, the swap button can be clicked to exchange the tile for three others. When a player runs out of tiles and no tiles care left to be given, the finish button can be clicked to be scored. The scoring function determines if all of the player's tiles form a continuous block of English words. If successful, the player wins. Otherwise, the player's possible winning score is decreased.
